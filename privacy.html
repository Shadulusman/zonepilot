Privacy Policy

Effective: April 27, 2026

ZonePilot is built around a simple principle: your run data is yours. This policy explains what data the app handles, why, and where it goes. Short answer: it stays on your phone.

1. Who we are

ZonePilot (“the app”) is operated by Shad Ulusman (“we”, “us”). You can reach us via the support page.

2. What ZonePilot accesses

To do its job, ZonePilot requests permission to use the following on your iPhone:

  • Bluetooth. To connect to your heart rate monitor (WHOOP, Polar, Garmin, etc.) and read live heart rate values.
  • Location (GPS). To track distance, pace, and route while you run. Location is only read while a run is active. Background location is used so tracking continues with the screen locked.
  • Microphone & Audio Output. ZonePilot does not record audio. It uses the audio output to speak voice cues over your music or headphones.
  • Local Storage. Completed runs (route, splits, heart rate, duration, distance) are saved on your device.

3. Data we collect

ZonePilot does not require an account. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any identifying information to use the app.

The data the app handles — your heart rate readings, GPS track, splits, lap times, and run history — stays on your iPhone. We do not upload it to any server we control.

4. Data we share

We do not share your data with third parties. We do not sell, rent, or license it to anyone. We have no advertising partners and run no ad networks.

The only time data leaves your device is if you explicitly export or share it — for example, by tapping the share button to send a run summary card to a friend or social network. In that case, the destination app (Messages, Instagram, etc.) handles the data under their privacy policy, not ours.

5. Analytics & tracking

ZonePilot does not use third-party analytics SDKs (no Google Analytics, no Firebase, no Mixpanel, no Amplitude). We do not include the IDFA tracking framework. We do not fingerprint your device.

The word “analytics” in the app refers to your run analytics — lap breakdowns, time-in-zone, heart rate drift — computed locally for you to review. None of it is transmitted off-device.

6. Children’s privacy

ZonePilot is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Your rights

Because your run data lives only on your device, you control it directly:

  • Delete a run. Open History → tap the run → delete.
  • Delete everything. Uninstalling ZonePilot removes all locally stored runs and settings.
  • Revoke permissions. Manage Bluetooth, Location, and Microphone access in iOS Settings → ZonePilot at any time.

8. Security

Run data is stored using iOS’ standard local storage, which benefits from the device-level encryption iOS applies when you have a passcode set. We strongly recommend keeping a passcode and Face ID/Touch ID enabled on your iPhone.

9. Changes to this policy

If we change how the app handles data — for example, if we add an optional cloud sync or export integration — we will update this page and revise the “Effective” date above. Changes that broaden what data is collected will be highlighted in the app itself before they take effect.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Use the support page to send us a message.
