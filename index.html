How it works

  1. Pair your monitor. Open your WHOOP/Polar/Garmin app and enable Bluetooth heart rate broadcast. ZonePilot auto-detects.
  2. Set your zones. Enter your max HR or let ZonePilot estimate it. Five zones calibrate instantly.
  3. Slide to start. Pop in your headphones, lock your phone, and run. ZonePilot speaks up only when you need to hear something.
  4. Review the run. See your map, splits, time-in-zone, and HR drift. Share a card if you want.
